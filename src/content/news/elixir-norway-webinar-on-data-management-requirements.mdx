**Be ready to receive funding! Why is Data Management important for my application?**

This webinar on the 5th of February (10.00 CET) from
[ELIXIR Norway](https://elixir.no/) will provide you with information on
Data Management requirements for research applications to the Research
Council of Norway (RCN) call for proposals with deadline February 10th.

The RCN requires that all funded projects must assess the need for a
Data Management Plan. In this webinar we will highlight the RCN
requirements for Data Management from a Life Science perspective and
give a short introduction to the Data Management Planning tool
"[Data Stewardship Wizard](https://elixir-no.ds-wizard.org/)" by ELIXIR Norway.
