ELIXIR Norway contributed to hosting the 2-day **"AlphaFold2 and RoseTTAFold workshop"** with 600 participants from 40 countries.

NORA (Norwegian Artificial Intelligence Research Consortium), in a collaboration with ELIXIR Norway, the University of Oslo, dScience, Oslo Cancer Cluster, and the Centre for Digital Life Norway, hosted an open workshop on new artificial intelligence-based methods for protein structure prediction on [Aug 31st and Sept 1st, 2021](https://www.nora.ai/events/AlphaFoldv2.0-and-RoseTTAFold-workshop%20.html). This ended up as a world-wide event with participants joining from 40 countries, from New Zealand to Peru.

Read more about the event, and find links to video recordings of all presentations, [here](https://www.nora.ai/news-and-events/news/protein-folding-workshop.html).
