Each participating Node will apply new skills to a self-selected “impact challenge” — a case study evaluating the impact of specific activities relevant to their national context and stakeholders. Findings and good practices will be shared across ELIXIR to benefit the wider community.

The main goal is to enhance each Node’s ability to demonstrate and communicate its public value to funders and stakeholders, supporting long-term sustainability. The project also aims to strengthen the growing ELIXIR community of practice in impact evaluation, aligning with 2020 ESFRI recommendations on sustainability of research infrastructures.
