---
title: "ELIXIR-STEERS"
summary: "ELIXIR-STEERS aims to help life science researchers to access national data sets and conduct large-scale, cross-border analysis of data from across Europe.
It will promote good software management practices and support life scientists with their software management needs.
It will collect these good practices into a toolkit for green and reproducible software and workflows.
Norways contribution is in  WP4: Strengthening and equipping ELIXIR Nodes and Node staff with key skills and resources in organisation, management and training"
status: "european"
period: "1 February 2024 - 31 January 2027"
