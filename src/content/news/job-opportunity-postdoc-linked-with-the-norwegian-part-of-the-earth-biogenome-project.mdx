**Exciting Postdoctoral opportunity linked with the Norwegian of the Earth
Biogenome Project and ELIXIR Norway!**

The [Earth BioGenome Project (EBP)](https://www.earthbiogenome.org) is
a global non-profit initiative with the aim to catalogue the genomes of
the entirety of all of Earth's 1\.5 million eukaryotic species over a
period of ten years. The Norwegian national network node of EBP, EBPNor,
has the aim to sequence all Norwegian eukaryotic species
(estimated to 45,000 in total).

Read more and [**apply by 30 Jan 2022 here**](https://www.jobbnorge.no/en/available-jobs/job/216789/postdoctoral-research-fellow-position-within-informatics-bioinformatics)!
