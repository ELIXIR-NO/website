Dr [Federico Bianchini](https://www.mn.uio.no/bils/english/people/fredebi/),
Senior Engineer at the Department for Biosciences at the Univesity of Oslo (UiO) and long-time member of ELIXIR Norway,
has been appointed as interim co-lead of the  ELIXIR Research Data Management (RDM) Community for 2026.

The [ELIXIR RDM Community](https://elixir-europe.org/communities/research-data-management) supports topics including
Data Management Plans (DMPs), data brokering, ontologies, metadata standards, AI-readiness, RDM policies, and other emerging areas,
and will evolve over time in response to technological advances and stakeholder needs.

With over 250 registered members, the Community is active, engaged and professionally supportive,
benefiting both individual members and their institutions.
Community members contribute to key RDM ecosystem resources, including the [RDMkit](https://rdmkit.elixir-europe.org/), [FAIR Cookbook](https://faircookbook.elixir-europe.org/content/home.html), [Data Stewardship Wizard (DSW)](https://ds-wizard.org/), [TeSS](https://tess.elixir-europe.org/), and the [Data Stewardship Handbook](https://elixir-europe.github.io/ds-handbook/).
These resources provide practical guidance and best practices, and are widely used by RDM professionals in the life sciences.

Bianchini, who is already a member of the editorial board of RDMkit, an annotator on TeSS, a power user of DSW,
and a liason to FAIRsharing via the [Community Champions Programme](https://fairsharing.org/community_champions),
sees this position as a direct continuation of his line of work and as a formal recognition of the efforts of ELIXIR Norway over the years on the thematic
areas of Research Data Management and FAIR.
