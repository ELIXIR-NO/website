Sweden and SciLifeLab has launched a National Pathogen Portal to facilitate sharing of data related to pathogens and facilitate future pandemic preparedness. ELIXIR Norway has applied for national funding from the [Norwegian Research Council](https://www.forskningsradet.no/en/) to build a Norwegian Pathogen Portal.

Read more [here](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/about/news/updates-from-data-resources/extending-the-network-of-infectious-disease-data-portals-developments-in-sweden-and-the-netherlands/).
