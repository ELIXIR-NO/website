**Identifying gaps in current training**

ELIXIR invite you to participate in a survey in order to improve and
expand the current training portfolio.

The survey ([link here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSca-40-pxZIhgEL18bgqOnPYtsnn-7gNOq6d7QIwbFnUh7y-g/viewform?usp=sf_link))
should take no more than 5-10 minutes of your time to complete; questions with
an asterisk require a response, and it would be immensely helpful if you
could spare the time to respond fully to all questions.
