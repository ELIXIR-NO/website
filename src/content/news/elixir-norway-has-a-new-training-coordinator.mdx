**From October 1st. Erik Hjerde took over the role as the Training
coordinator for ELIXIR Norway.**

ELIXIR Training is organised through its [Training Platform](https://elixir-europe.org/platforms/training).
The Training Platform is a European network of trainers. Each national ELIXIR node
is represented in the Training Platform through the national training coordinator.
The role of the Training coordinator is among other, to disseminate
information about international training events to Norwegian scientists,
and to make national training events available to the international scientists.

Information about our training events are collected
in [TeSS](https://tess.elixir-europe.org/) (ELIXIR's Training Portal)

If you are interested in either participate in international ELIXIR
training events, or to discuss the possibility of making your course
available through ELIXIR, please
contact [contact@bioinfo.no](mailto:contact@bioinfo.no)
